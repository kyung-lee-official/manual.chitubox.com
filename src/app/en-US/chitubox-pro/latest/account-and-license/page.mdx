# Account and License Key

## Account Registration

Please click<Link href="https://cc.chitubox.com/register"> here </Link>and sign up if you have not registered a CHITUBOX account.

## License Key

An account can have multiple license keys. The license key will be automatically linked to your account when you make a purchase. This license key cannot be unbound from the account.

A personal version of license key can be used on up to two computers. A team version of the license key can be used on up to 5 computers. You can log onto user center anytime to unlink your computers.

<Admonition type="danger" title="Warning">
	Please do not share your license key with anybody and bind it to your
	account right after you receive it.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="note" title="Attention">
	CHITUBOX Pro uses online account verification. Please ensure that your
	computer is connected to the internet.
</Admonition>

### Unbind License Keys

You will need to unbind your license key from your current computer if you want to migrate CHITUBOX to a new machine.

Log into your <Link href="https://cc.chitubox.com/login">CHITUBOX account</Link> on web.

Click on **My Products** > **My License Keys**

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-add-your-license-to-your-account-006.png"
	}
/>

Click on **Managed Linked PC** > **Unlink**

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-add-your-license-to-your-account-007.png"
	}
/>

Your license key will be unbound from your current computer, and then you can re-activate the key on a new machine.

You can also perform unbinding in the CHITUBOX software.

### Replace License Key

You will need to manually replace a license key upon expiration.

<Entry mainMenu={["Account", "Authorization"]} />

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/2.x.x/account-and-license-management-002.png"
