## Hollow

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-prepare008.png"
	}
/>

Hollow out selected models according to the set hollowing parameters.

<ImageContainer
	src={"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/gif/003hollow.gif"}
	style={{ maxWidth: 200 }}
/>

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-prepare008.1.png"
	}
/>

-   Wall Thickness: Thickness of the chamber wall after an object is hollowed (thickness of peeled-off part under **Outer** mode).

-   Mode:

    -   _Inner:_ Hollowing the object into a closed chamber.

    -   _Outer:_ Peel off the a layer set by **Wall Thickness**.

    -   _Bottom through:_ Similar to _Inner mode_, but also removes the bottom part of the model.

-   Infill: Enable/Disable generating infill structures.

-   Precision: Density of generated triangles. A value of 0 represents the minimum density, and 1 represents the maximum.

-   Hollowing process animation: Play the animation when hollowing (refer to progress bar at the bottom for the real progress).

-   Keep the main cavity: Only one main cavity is generated when the model is hollowed out.

## Dig Hole

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-prepare009.png"
	}
/>

Generate holes on selected models with custom-set parameters.

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-prepare009.1.png"
	}
/>

-   Hole Mode: Choose between generating Penerating or Non-penetrating hole.

-   Edge Number: As number of edge increases, the shape of the hole will get close to a circle.

-   Î¦: The diameter of the hole.

-   B: The maximum depth of the hole for non-penetrating holes.

-   C: The angle between the side wall of the hole and the bottom plane of the hole.

-   Rotate: The rotation angle of the hole.

-   Keep Hole: Whether to keep the excavated material as a separate part.

-   Angle: Digging angle perpendicular to the screen or to the model.

-   Forced digging hole: Turning On/Off Forced Hole Generation.

-   Limited Depth B: Turning On/Off limiting hole depth as defined by B under Penetrating Model.
