## Edit Pixels

Add or delete pixels in the current slice file.

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-slice-preview011.png"
	}
/>

1. Hold Ctrl and scroll the Middle-button of the mouse to resize the pen; left-click to add pixels; right-click to remove pixels.

1. Hold Ctrl and drag Left-button of the mouse to add continuous pixels. Hold Ctrl and drag Right-button to continuously erase pixels.

## Format Brush

Copy the format of the target pixel and apply to other pixels.

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-slice-preview012.png"
	}
/>

1. Left-click a pixel as the target pixel format.

1. Scroll the Middle-button to adjust the size of brush.

1. Hold the Left-button and move mouse to draw, click Right-button to finish the operation.
