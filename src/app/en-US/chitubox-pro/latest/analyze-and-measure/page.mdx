## Two Point Distance

<TwoPointDistance /> Measures and displays the distance between two points on the model.

## Point to Plane Distance

<PointToPlaneDistance /> Measures and displays the distance between a point and a plane on the model.

## Parallel Plane Distance

<ParallelPlaneDistance /> Measures and displays the distance between two parallel planes on the model.
