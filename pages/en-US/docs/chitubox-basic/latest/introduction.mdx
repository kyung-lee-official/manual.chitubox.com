# Introduction

CHITUBOX Basic is a free 3D printing software (slicer) that slices and converts 3D mesh files into printable files. It is designed to make 3D printing easier and more efficient, simplifies the entire 3D printing process, allowing users to quickly and easily convert 3D models into 3D prints. The software supports a variety of 3D printing machines and profiles, and it also offers a wide range of options for optimizing models for 3D printing.
