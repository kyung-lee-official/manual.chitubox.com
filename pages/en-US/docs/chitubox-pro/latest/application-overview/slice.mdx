# Slice

## Single Parameter and Multi-parameter

Slice is the final step before your sliced file is generated.

Choose a **Single Parameter Slice** or **Multi-parameter Slice** to and click the button to enter slice preview.

	Resin Volume is calculated by summing up voxels (area of pixel $\times$
	layer height), the algorithm is pretty simple. However, this is a
	theoretical value, it could be affected by many other factors (resin residue
	on the build plate and model surface, volatility, density change during
	curing, etc.) in the real world.
</Admonition>

### Single Parameter Slice

**Single Parameter Slice** slices your models with the selected default profile in the **Slicing Profile Panel**. Applied to all your models inside the printable space on the platform.

### Multi-parameter Slice

<Admonition type="note">
	Not all printers support **Multi-parameter**.
</Admonition>

**Multi-parameter Slice** provides 3 modes, **Model**, **Height**, and **Cross-sectional Area**.

#### 1. Model

Different parameters for separate models. <br />

#### 3. Cross-sectional Area

Different parameters for graded cross-sectional areas. The percentage here refers to the percentage of the total effective area of the cross-section relative to the platform.

1. Sections without specified Profile comply with the Profile specified by **Default Profile**.

1. Parameters of bottom layers and transition layers comply with the Profile specified by **Default Profile**.

1. When editing exclusive parameters, non editable items (marked by **`*`**) will use default Profile.
