# Slice Preview

## Single Parameter and Multi-parameter

Slice is the final step before your sliced file is generated.

Choose a **Single Parameter Slice** or **Multi-parameter Slice** to and click the button to enter slice preview.

	Resin Volume is calculated by summing up voxels (area of pixel $\times$

	curing, etc.) in the real world.
</Admonition>

### Single Parameter Slice

**Single Parameter Slice** slices your models with the selected default profile in the **Slicing Profile Panel**. Applied to all your models inside the printable space on the platform.

## Edit Pixels

Add or delete pixels in the current slice file.

<StyledAntdImage
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-slice-preview011.png"
	}
/>

1. Hold Ctrl and scroll the Middle-button of mouse to resize the pen, click Left-button to add pixels, Right-button to erase pixels.

1. Hold Ctrl and drag Left-button to add continuous pixels. Hold Ctrl and drag Right-button to continuously erase pixels.

1. Hold Ctrl and scroll the Middle-button can resize the pen.

## Format Brush

Copy the format of the target pixel, apply to other pixels.

<StyledAntdImage
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/006-application-overview-slice-preview012.png"
	}
/>

1. Left-click a pixel as the target pixel format.

1. Scroll the Middle-button to adjust the size of brush.

1. Hold the Left-button and move mouse to draw, click Right-button to finish the operation.

## Network Sending

<Admonition type="note">
	1. Your printer should have a USB drive plugged in to store received sliced files from your computer.
	1. Your printer and computer must under the same LAN.
	1. The transmission speed is limited by printer's network module, for most printers, it's around 500KBytes/s, therefore for large size files, **Network Sending** is not recommended.
	1. Not all printers support **Network Sending**. Read the <SpanA href="https://wkhvloo2kw.feishu.cn/docx/Q5nbdGSnfo5EbAxgEwKccN0Rnxe">ðŸ“ƒfull compatibility list</SpanA> here.
</Admonition>
