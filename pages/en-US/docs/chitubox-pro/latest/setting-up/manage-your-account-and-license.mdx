# Manage Your Account and License

## Activate Your License

<Admonition type="danger">
	You should **NEVER** tell anyone your license serial number and should bind
	your license to your account as soon as you get it.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="note">
	Make sure your PC is connected to the Internet. The personal license supports two devices, and the team license supports five devices.
</Admonition>

When CHITUBOX Pro is launched for the first time, the Login window should show up.

If you have not installed CHITUBOX Pro, please download the latest version <SpanA href="https://www.chitubox.com/en/download/chitubox-pro">here</SpanA>.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-login002.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Click <SpanA href="https://cc.chitubox.com/register">Register</SpanA> if you don't have an account.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-activate-license001.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Click **Activate License** and input your license:

<Admonition type="note">
	CHITUBOX will check your account after login, if your account doesn't have the permission for trial, **Start Trial** will not display.
</Admonition>

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-activate-license002.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Click **Next**, your license will be activated, and bind to your PC.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-activate-license003.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

If the license associates with a printer, the ID of the printer is also required.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-activate-license004.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

If you <SpanA href="https://cc.chitubox.com/login">sign in</SpanA> to your CHITUBOX account on web, you should be able to see your license under **My Products** > **My Licenses**.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-add-your-license-to-your-account-005.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

If you already entered CHITUBOX Pro with a trial license, click <NextLink href="/en-US/docs/chitubox-pro/latest/application-overview/setting-and-help#license">here</NextLink> to see where you can update your license.

## Migrate to Your New PC

If your PC was damaged accidently, or you want to migrate CHITUBOX to your new PC, you'll need to unbind your license from your old PC.

<SpanA href="https://cc.chitubox.com/login">Sign in</SpanA> to your CHITUBOX account on web.

Click **My Products** > **My Licenses**,

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-add-your-license-to-your-account-006.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Click **Binding PC Management** > **Unbind**

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		src={
			"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-add-your-license-to-your-account-007.png"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Your license would be unbinded from your old PC. Then you can <NextLink href="#activate-your-license">activate</NextLink> your license in your new PC.

## Replace the license

If the license expires, you need to replace the license serial number manually.

Click **Activate License**:

<AntdImage
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-activate-license005.png"
	}
/>

Then replace the license and click **Next**, your license will be activated, and bind to your PC.

	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/chitubox-pro/1.x.x/004-setting-up-activate-license006.png"
	}
